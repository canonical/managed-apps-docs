Managed Services
=================

Canonical Managed Services gives you effortless operational excellence for your
cloud-native application ecosystem. Our end-to-end, single-vendor managed
portfolio lets you focus on making the most of your tools — whatever they are
and wherever they run — without worrying about operations.

What is Managed Services?
-------------------------

Managed Apps is a fully managed service for cloud-native applications running
on-premises or on public clouds. Canonical engineers work around the clock to
deliver monitoring, management, troubleshooting and security enforcement for
your application stack, under market-standard SLAs.

App portfolio
-------------

MLOps
~~~~~

Automate your machine learning operations at any scale with fully managed
Kubeflow and MLFlow.

Data processing
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Deploy high-performance Apache Kafka or Spark clusters and leave the rest to us.

Search & analytics
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Enjoy hassle-free OpenSearch for any use case.

SQL databases
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Go in depth with extensible and compliant tools like PostgreSQL, minding only
the data.

Landscape
~~~~~~~~~

Manage your Ubuntu machines at scale without worrying about tooling, with
`Landscape <https://ubuntu.com/landscape/managed>`_.

Kubernetes
~~~~~~~~~~

Run production-grade Kubernetes clusters managed end-to-end by Canonical.

Operational coverage
--------------------

**Monitoring** — Our in-house observability toolkit alongside our engineers'
watchful eyes ensure your systems are correctly and completely monitored.

**Management** — Upgrades and patches are handled for you: knowing when to
apply a new release and taking all precautions before doing so.

**Troubleshooting** — Military-grade incident and recovery protocols give you
quick functionality restoration and extensive root-cause investigations.

**Security enforcement** — Obsessive attention to compliance, security
regulations, and legal restrictions, ensuring your stacks are protected against
even the greatest threats.

Natively multi-cloud
--------------------

Our managed app portfolio is natively multi-cloud, allowing you to deploy, run,
and harness the power of your applications both on-premises and on all major
public clouds:

* :doc:`Azure <azure/index>`
* :doc:`AWS <aws/index>`
* :doc:`GCP <gcp/index>`
* :doc:`OpenStack <openstack/index>`

Service options
---------------

**Managed Services** — Regain your space to innovate and leave all operations
to us. Includes 24/7 end-to-end operational coverage, proactive and reactive
excellence, and market-standard SLAs.

**Firefighting Support** — Engineers on call to resolve the most critical
issues while you manage your own environment. Includes a 1-hour response time
via video call for high-severity situations.
